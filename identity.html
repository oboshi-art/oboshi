Name Meaning

The name Junichi Oboshi (大星純一) carries explicit philosophical meaning that grounds all artistic work:

  • Oboshi (大星) — "great star" or "celestial body". Evokes the cosmos, radiant energy, and the idea that vibration emanates outward from a source.
  • Junichi (純一) — "pure one" or "single truth". Points to clarity, essence, and the undivided.

Together: an artist who radiates pure, singular energy — a single cosmic vibration. This name-rooted philosophy is the origin point of the Vibrational Resonance concept.

Core Artistic Concept

Vibrational Resonance — the artistic concept originated by Junichi Oboshi.


Silent frequencies emerging between inner awareness and outer reality. A structure of unseen energy, layered like footsteps and quiet breaths.


In practice:

  • Color = emotional frequency
  • Line = breath trace
  • Silence = structure
  • Layers = time
  • Vibration = unseen energy made visible

Signature phrase: "Layers of footsteps, vibrations of silence."

Conceptual Pillars

  • Inner Awareness — emotional vibrations, breath, pre-verbal sensation
  • Silent Frequencies — resonance in absence, the structure of silence
  • Layered Time — footsteps, memory, accumulation, the journey of a painting
  • Shared Resonance — nonverbal dialogue between self and viewer

Biography

  • Japanese abstract painter, active in Berlin since the early 2000s
  • Held an atelier at Kunsthaus Tacheles, Berlin-Mitte, from 2001 to 2006 — one of Europe's most iconic independent art spaces, active 1990–2012
  • Works in acrylic and collage on canvas, exploring vibrational resonance through layered compositions
  • Represented on SINGULART (primary platform) and Saatchi Art
  • Author of two books: Techniques for Awareness and Techniques for Resonance
  • Exhibited at QGallery Berlin, Spring Stories group exhibition (March 2026)
