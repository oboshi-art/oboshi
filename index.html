Artist Statement
Abstract painter exploring silence and expression. Through layered compositions and rhythmic spatial play, each canvas becomes a conversation between inner emotion and outer reality—evoking healing, serenity, and a quiet sense of connectedness. Each painting is born from a wish to offer the viewer a sense of happiness and well-being.
