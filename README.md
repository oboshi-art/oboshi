## What this is

This website was built in dialogue between the artist Junichi Oboshi (OBOSHI) and an AI — across hundreds of conversations spanning code, philosophy, and the writing of artwork descriptions.

Every description of every painting was written fresh, from direct visual reading, shaped by OBOSHI's philosophy of Vibrational Resonance.

The Talk system embedded in each artwork modal speaks from within the practice — not about it.

This is not a website about art. It is an extension of the work itself.

---

## The artist

OBOSHI (Junichi Oboshi / 大星純一) is a Japanese abstract acrylic painter based in Berlin since 2001. He held an early atelier at Kunsthaus Tacheles (2001–2006) and is the author of two books: *Techniques for Awareness* and *Techniques for Resonance*.

His work explores Vibrational Resonance — silent frequencies emerging between inner awareness and outer reality, structured through layers of acrylic and collage on canvas.
